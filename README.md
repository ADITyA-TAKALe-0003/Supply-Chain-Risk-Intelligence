📦 Supply Chain Risk Intelligence Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes operational risk across the supply chain using inventory health, supplier performance, regional exposure, and transport modes.

The goal was to identify hidden risk drivers using lead time, turnover, and stock status indicators — and convert raw operational data into executive-level insights.

🎯 Business Objective
Identify high-risk suppliers
Detect regional exposure concentration
Analyze transport mode impact on risk
Understand inventory health using turnover & lead time
Highlight stock imbalance (Low Stock / Overstock)

📊 Dashboard Components
1️⃣ Inventory Turnover by Product Type (Risk Highlighted)
Stacked bar visualization
Risk categories:
🟢 Healthy
🔴 Low Stock
🟡 Overstock
Helps detect product-level imbalanc

2️⃣ Inventory Risk Drivers (Scatter Analysis)
X-axis → Lead Time
Y-axis → Inventory Turnover
Color → Stock Risk
This helps analyze:
Whether longer lead times are causing stockouts
Whether low turnover is creating overstock situations
This chart adds operational root-cause analysis.

3️⃣ Supplier Risk Ranking
Ranked by Composite Risk Score
Identifies suppliers contributing most to overall supply chain vulnerability

4️⃣ Region Risk Overview
Compares composite risk across cities
Detects geographical concentration of supply chain risk

5️⃣ Transport Mode Risk
Compares risk levels across Road, Air, Rail, Sea
Helps evaluate logistics strategy effectiveness

💡 Key Insights
Certain suppliers consistently show elevated composite risk.
Some regions display higher operational vulnerability.
Lead time strongly impacts stock health.
Overstocks and stockouts coexist across product categories — indicating imbalance.
